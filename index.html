ShakeToShare

ShakeToShare is a way to send contact information by shaking hands (using Pebble smartwatches and Android). This product was created at Wildhacks 2014 and won the Microsoft Sponsor prize at the competition. The hack can also be modified to send payment information by venmo or other technologies by using different gestures (this wasn't implemented at the hackathon due to the time constraint).
